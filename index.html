We Turn Events Into Unforgettable Memories

Founded in 2017, The Photobooth Co. has been dedicated to bringing excitement, entertainment, and unforgettable memories to every event we serve. What started as a passion for capturing life's happiest moments has grown into a trusted photo booth provider for corporate events, weddings, private celebrations, schools, government functions, and brand activations across the Philippines.

For nearly a decade, we have continuously evolved by investing in modern technology, premium equipment, and innovative photo booth experiences that keep up with today's event industry. Our commitment to quality, creativity, and exceptional customer service has earned us the trust of countless clients and event organizers.

At The Photobooth Co., we believe every smile tells a story. Our mission is to capture those genuine moments and transform them into lasting memories that guests can cherish forever.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional photo booth experiences through innovative technology, creative solutions, and outstanding customer service, while making every celebration more engaging, memorable, and enjoyable.

Our Vision

To become one of the Philippines' leading and most trusted event entertainment providers, recognized for creativity, professionalism, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
